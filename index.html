How are we different?

OZŪ is an app where friends can add and subtract amounts that they owe each other (on trips for instance) instead of constantly sending each other various payments back and forth. Final payments can be totaled at the end of the trip to see what each friend actually owes, and transfers can be made easily within the app.

Payments can also be split for large purchases and recurring bills can be set up as well for things like rent so there’s no awkward monthly confrontation involved anymore.
